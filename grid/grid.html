Clouds

Clouds are fluffy, floating masses of water vapor in the sky. They come in various shapes and sizes, ranging from wispy to puffy. Sometimes they look like cotton candy, and other times like soft blankets covering the blue sky. Clouds bring rain, shade, and imagination to our world, constantly changing their forms as they drift lazily above us.

Windows

Global Market Share Percentages: 78%

Windows is a popular operating system created by Microsoft. It's known for its user-friendly interface and compatibility with various software and hardware. Regular updates ensure security and smooth functioning, making it widely used in both personal and professional settings.

Mac

Global Market Share Percentages: 20%

Mac, developed by Apple Inc., is recognized for its sleek design and seamless integration across Apple devices. Its operating system, macOS, offers a smooth user experience with built-in productivity tools like Pages and Keynote. Macs are favored by creative professionals for their performance and reliability.

Linux

Global Market Share Percentages: 2%

Linux is an open-source operating system with numerous distributions catering to different needs. It's known for stability, security, and flexibility, making it popular for servers and specialized applications. Despite a learning curve, Linux offers extensive customization and control options, appealing to tech enthusiasts and developers.
